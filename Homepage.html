Design Fundamentals Website

As part of my coursework project I have completed a Typographic Systems Project, featuring 8 designs, 1 being in each of the following typographic systems; Axial, Radial, Dilatatonal, Random, Grid, Modular, Transitional and Bilateral.

As part of my coursework project I also completed a CARP Project, where I designed 3 posters for the Girls Skate Clinic, with 1 being heavily focussed on. These posters contain a heavy focus on the graphic design principles of Contrast, Alignment, Repetition and Proximity.

As part of my coursework proect I also completed 5 Coding Worksheets, which helped teach the foundations of HTML and CSS and how to create a flexbox website in HTMl and CSS.

As part of my coursework project I also completed a 500 word reflective essay report in which I reflect upon each element of my coursework and dive into the effectiveness of my Typographic Systems Project, CARP Project and flexbox website.
